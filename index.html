The Pattern

Most portfolio companies have the same product problems.

The acquisition closes. The value-creation thesis is clear. Then execution stalls — not because the team is wrong, but because the product operating model was never built to scale.

01
Reactive product dynamics
Roadmaps driven by the loudest customer or last board meeting. No system for translating strategy into sequenced execution.
02
Low-leverage bandwidth consumption
PMs spending half their week on documentation and status work that adds no product judgment. AI tools adopted but not operationalized.
03
AI strategy without execution
Every org has slides about transformation. Almost none have a system that changes how the team operates day to day.
04
No product operating model
Growth inflection exposes the absence of repeatable processes, clear ownership, and the infrastructure needed to scale output without scaling headcount.
