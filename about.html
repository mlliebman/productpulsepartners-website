About

25 years building and fixing product organizations at the moments that matter.

Over 25 years I've led product organizations inside founder-led startups, PE-backed growth companies, and mid-market B2B SaaS businesses — from early product-market fit through the growth inflections that expose every gap in the operating model.

I've run due diligence for Advent International, Blackstone, Citation Capital, and Oakley Capital. I've served as embedded CPO at Thoma Bravo, Genstar Capital, and Insight Partners portfolio companies — sitting inside the business, owning the roadmap, and building the product operating model from the ground up.

Across those roles I've managed $300M+ in annual revenue, optimized 15+ product lines, led 40+ product managers, and integrated 10+ acquisitions. Thirty-plus due diligence projects and over $1B in exit value generated along the way.

I specialize in growth inflections. The moment after acquisition when the value-creation thesis has to become a real operating plan. That's where I've spent my career, and it's where I do my best work.

What sets me apart from every other fractional CPO in the market: I bring the AI infrastructure with me. I've built and deployed a purpose-built agentic workflow platform that runs inside the product organizations I work with. The results show up in team output, roadmap quality, and EBITDA — not in a slide deck.

I take a small number of engagements at any one time. That's deliberate. Every client gets precise attention — not a team of associates, not a playbook run on autopilot.
