About

When product execution determines the outcome, experience is the only edge.

Over 25 years I've led product organizations inside founder-led startups, growth-stage companies, and mid-market B2B SaaS businesses — from early product-market fit through the inflections that expose every gap in the operating model.

I've run technical and product due diligence across some of the most active investment firms in the market. I've served as embedded Chief Product Officer at portfolio companies across the full investment lifecycle — sitting inside the business, owning the roadmap, and building the product operating model from the ground up.

I work at both ends of the transaction. Pre-close, I give deal teams a precise read on what they're buying — product viability, team capability, technology risk, and commercial exposure. Post-close, I build the operating model the value-creation thesis requires.

What I bring that goes beyond the engagement: the AI infrastructure. I've built a purpose-built agentic workflow platform for product teams and I deploy it where the work calls for it. The results show up in output quality, roadmap precision, and EBITDA.

I take a small number of engagements at any one time. That's deliberate. Every client gets direct access to me — not a team of associates nor a playbook handed off to someone else.
