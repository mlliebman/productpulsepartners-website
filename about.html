Scaling High-Performing Teams: A track record of building and scaling high-performing organizations, directly leading 40+ product management FTEs while streamlining development and enhancing speed to market.

Driving Measurable Value: Expert in scaling product-market fit, accelerating revenue growth, and driving EBITDA expansion through strategic execution and operational rigor.

Data, AI & GTM Focus: Passionate about embedding AI-driven insights and data strategies to fuel innovation, while synchronizing product strategy with sales, retention, and go-to-market initiatives to drive long-term growth.

Our Mission

Product Pulse Partners exists to help private equity investors and C-suite leaders make smarter, faster decisions in the most critical moments—whether you're evaluating an acquisition, optimizing a portfolio company, or navigating a complex product or AI transformation.

We are operators, not theorists. With 25+ years of hands-on experience building, scaling, and exiting B2B SaaS companies, we bring the strategic clarity, technical depth, and execution mindset that bridge the gap between vision and results.

  • For Investors: We reduce risk in M&A with deep technical and product due diligence, uncover hidden value in portfolio companies, and provide the strategic roadmap to accelerate growth and maximize exits.
  • For C-Suite Leaders: We provide fractional CPO leadership, embed AI and data strategy, build high-performing product organizations, and execute against your most ambitious transformation goals.

Our approach is grounded in real-world execution: we've led product teams through PE-backed exits, built organizations from the ground up, and understand the pressures you face, the questions that keep you up at night, and what it takes to deliver measurable results.
